About Ram Mandir

The Ram Mandir in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, is a grand Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ram. It is located at the site believed to be the birthplace of Lord Ram, making it one of the most revered religious sites in India. The temple showcases stunning traditional Nagara-style architecture, symbolizing India's rich cultural and spiritual heritage.

History of Ram Mandir

The history of the Ram Mandir dates back centuries, with its significance deeply rooted in Hindu mythology and scriptures. According to the Ramayana, Ayodhya is the birthplace of Lord Ram, the seventh avatar of Lord Vishnu. The site has been a focal point of devotion and historical events for centuries.
